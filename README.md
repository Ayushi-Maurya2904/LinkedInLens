## 🎯 The Problem

Most LinkedIn creators struggle with 4 things:

| Problem | Reality |
|---|---|
| **The Hook** | Can't stop the scroll in the first line |
| **The Profile** | Unsure if their profile converts views into opportunities |
| **The Consistency** | Time-consuming to draft, refine, and publish quality posts daily |
| **The Why** | Don't understand why some posts perform brilliantly while others fail |

**Most AI tools only generate content. They don't teach you anything.**

LinkedInLens is different — it's a coach, not a generator.

---

## ✨ What LinkedInLens Does

| Standard AI | LinkedInLens |
|---|---|
| Just generates text | Analyses your drafts |
| Ignores your personal voice | Provides instant, actionable guidance |
| Offers zero feedback | Scores readability and structure |
| Teaches you nothing | Helps you understand *why* content works |

---

## 🛠️ 4 AI Tools — Live Now

### 🧲 Hook Generator
- Input your topic → Get **10 hook variations**
- Covers 5 psychological angles: Story, Curiosity, Achievement, Beginner, Technical
- Teaches you *why* each hook works differently

### ✨ Post Improver
- Input your raw draft → Get a polished version
- Checks readability, formatting, CTA strength, and structure
- Explains every improvement made

### 🔍 Profile Analyser
- Input your headline and about section
- Get: Overall score, missing keywords, structural suggestions, improved headline
- Identifies exactly what's stopping your profile from converting

### 📋 Post Reviewer
- Input your draft → Get scored before you post
- Metrics: Hook Strength, Readability Score, Engagement Potential
- Actionable coaching tips with colour-coded feedback
